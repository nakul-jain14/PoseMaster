Welcome to My PoseNet Project

Welcome to my Pose Detection Showcase, PoseMaster! This innovative project combines the power of machine learning with real-time video processing to bring you an engaging and interactive experience.

By harnessing the capabilities of the PoseNet library, My application detects a person's pose in real-time through a webcam. The pose is represented by key points and visualized through a dynamic stick figure overlay on the video stream.

My project is specifically trained to recognize four alphabet poses: Y, A, M, and C. When you strike one of these poses, the corresponding letter is displayed on the screen, demonstrating the accuracy and responsiveness of our machine learning model.

This showcase is a testament to the potential of machine learning in real-time video processing and human pose estimation. Explore, interact, and experience the future of AI-driven applications with my Pose Detection Showcase.
